# BookSpinner

A Vue 3 + Vite app. Users add books to a list, then spin a slot machine to pick the next book club read.

## Design Context

### Users
Casual book club members — friends and coworkers who gather regularly to discuss books. They use BookSpinner in a social, relaxed setting (living rooms, coffee shops, after work) to take the pressure off choosing the next read and make it feel fun. The app is used briefly during a meeting, often on a phone or laptop shared among a small group.

### Brand Personality
**Whimsical, cozy, playful.** Like a beloved indie bookshop that has a mischievous cat and hand-painted signs. There's warmth and welcome, but also a lightness — the slot machine spin is a moment of collective anticipation and delight, not a serious literary exercise. Words: *whimsical · cozy · playful*.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode — warm, daytime, social-setting appropriate
- **Direction**: Full redesign — new fonts, colors, layout. Move away from the current generic warm-serif template toward something more characterful and distinctive.
- **Visual tone**: Think a hand-illustrated children's book about libraries, or a quirky indie zine about reading. Not a luxury literary journal — more like a beloved paperback with a dog-eared cover.
- **The spin is the hero**: The SlotMachine component deserves the most visual energy. The rest of the UI should set the stage for that moment.
- **Anti-reference**: Generic "bookish" aesthetic — Playfair + cream + burgundy is the cliché. Avoid looking like every other book app.

### Design Principles
1. **Delight lives in the details** — micro-interactions, tactile button feels, and the spin animation should all feel crafted, not default.
2. **Warmth without cliché** — cozy doesn't mean beige and burgundy. Find warmth through texture, composition, and type choices that feel genuinely chosen.
3. **The group moment matters** — design for the shared experience of a group watching the spinner together. Big, readable, theatrical.
4. **Whimsy earns trust** — playful touches (unexpected colors, fun copy, little surprises) make the app memorable and loved.
5. **Light and fast** — the app is used briefly in social settings; every interaction should feel instant and satisfying.
